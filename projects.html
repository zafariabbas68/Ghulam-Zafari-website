Forest Cover Dynamics in the Mediterranean Basin (1985-2022)

Comprehensive analysis of land cover transitions using Landsat and Sentinel-2 data. Implemented Random Forest classification with 92% accuracy, revealing a 17% net forest loss with regional variations.

Key Findings:

  • Southern regions experienced 23% greater deforestation than northern areas
  • Coastal zones showed strongest correlation between urbanization and forest loss
  • Identified 3 distinct transition patterns through cluster analysis
